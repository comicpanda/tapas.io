Submissions

While Tapas Media advocates the growth of independent creators, we wish to avoid any misunderstandings and disputes of similarities between your work and content on Tapas. Therefore, we do not accept general unsolicited submissions.

By submitting to Tapas Media anyway, you indicate that:

  • There is no obligation your submission will be reviewed.

  • You automatically waive any future claims of similar IP on Tapas Media. We receive a large volume of stories and inevitably, there may be similarities between some. This is not intentional.

  • You understand that you may not receive an immediate response. We are a small team and may not be able to get to your submission right away. Regardless, we appreciate your patience and will try to get back to you as soon as possible.

Alternatively, if you are a professional artists and/or writer you can send us a note with your accomplishments to submissions@tapasmedia.co, and we will reach out if interested. If you choose to send us unsolicited creative works such as concepts, manuscripts, art, or any other works, you acknowledge that Tapas Media is under no obligation to review or respond to said materials. In addition, you automatically waive any future claims of similar IP on Tapas Media.
