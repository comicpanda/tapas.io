Submissions

Tapas Media and its personnel do not accept unsolicited submissions. Please do not submit any materials or ideas. We will not review them. Period. The purpose of this policy is to avoid any misunderstandings and disputes of similarities between your work and content on Tapas.

If you do not choose to follow this policy and send your materials and ideas in anyway, the following terms will automatically apply to your submission regardless of what your letter might say.

You agree that:

  • There is no obligation your submission will be reviewed.

  • Your submission will automatically become property of Tapas Media with no compensation to you.

  • Tapas Media does not guarantee the confidentiality of your submission.

If you are a professional artists and/ or writer you can send us a note with your accomplishments, and we will reach out if interested.
