Overview

  • What is a Tapisode?

    A Tapasode is a bite-sized chunk of story you can consume in 5 to 10 minutes on your mobile device. Tapasodes can be "chunks" of a comic, novel, or a script.

  • Is Tapas available on multiple platforms?

    The Tapas app is available on iOS and Android.

  • How do I publish on Tapas?

    Get in touch! Please send an email to submissions@tapasmedia.co. If you are a professional artist and/or writer you can send us a note with your accomplishments, and we will reach out if interested. Please do not directly submit any novel or comic materials or story ideas. If you're a comic creator and would like to self-publish your work as a free series on our apps and web platform, check out www.tapas.io.

  • Is Tapas a Publisher?

    We are a mobile publishing platform, not a traditional publisher. We provide artists with tools to easily publish and share their novels, comics, and more.

Getting started

  • What do I need to get started?

    • A mobile device: This app is available for iOS and Android.

    • An account: You can preview a ton of content for free, but to really start munching on story snacks you’ll need to sign up for an account. Your account not only keeps track of books and comics you’ve read or bookmarked, but also lets us send you free gifts!

Coins, Keys, and Unlocking

  • How do coins work?

    Coins are the in-app currency, used to get keys.

    Updated about 2 hours ago

    Permalink

  • How do I get more coins?

    There are multiple ways to earn more coins. The quickest way is to buy more coins, so you continue the series you started. The second is inviting friends to download the app. Third, you can watch ads to earn more coins.

    Updated about 2 hours ago

    Permalink

  • How do keys work?

    Each key unlocks one episode of a particular series. Keys are purchased with coins, and are series-specific.

    Updated about 2 hours ago

    Permalink

  • How do I get more keys?

    Keys can be purchased with the coins you have bought or earned.

    Updated about 2 hours ago

    Permalink

  • Are there different keys?

    Yes, keys are series-specific. Buying a Key is like buying an episode of a story, with the added flexibility to unlock episodes in whichever order you wish.
